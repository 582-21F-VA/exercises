# Exercise: Validation

For this assignment, your task is to create and validate a registration
form. To register, users must provide their email address, date of
birth, username, and password. The validation criteria are as follows:

- Users can only register if they are at least 18 years old.
- The username cannot include spaces.
- The password cannot include the username.

Prevent form submission until all fields are valid. Provide positive
feedback as users type, and negative feedback when a control loses
focus.
